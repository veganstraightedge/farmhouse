Farmhouse Conf Sponsorship Prospectus

Sponsorship : $1000

Let’s keep it simple. For Farmhouse Conf 5 Sponsorships... Each one costs $1,000. No levels to pick from. No variation in the perks. No haggling with each other. Simply, you pay one thousand U.S. Dollars to be a sponsor for Farmhouse Conf 5 (November 2, 2013).

Your sponsorship will get you:

  1. 2 tickets to use or give away as promotion
  2. Your logo on the Farmhouse Conf 5 section of The Farmhouse website
  3. Your logo on screen in the Farmhouse Conf 5 videos
  4. Mentioned on mic to the attendees a couple few times during Farmhouse Conf 5
  5. The warm fuzzies for supporting such a different style event

Party Sponsorship : $2,500

This year’s after party will be absolutely banging. You’ll want to be a part of this. We’re pulling out all of the stops. This will be a party to remember. Which means we need a few sponsors to go big with us. Each party sponsorship is $2500 for Farmhouse Conf 5 (November 2, 2013).

Your party sponsorship will get you:

  1. 5 tickets to use or give away as promotion
  2. Your logo BIG on the Farmhouse Conf 5 section of The Farmhouse website
  3. Your logo BIG on screen by itself in the Farmhouse Conf 5 videos
  4. Especially thanked on mic to the attendees multiple times during Farmhouse Conf 5
  5. Totally righteous street cred for sponsoring the party of all parties

Speaker Sponsorship : $350

This year nearly every past speaker is returning to give short Alumni Updates. It's a great way to continue the thread of past confs/talks/speakers. And a perfect way to wrap this whole thing up. A lot of them are traveling on an airplane from another city. Which means we need some sponsors to help us cover their travel costs. Each party sponsorship is $350 for Farmhouse Conf 5 (November 2, 2013).

Your party sponsorship will get you:

  1. 1 ticket to use or give away as promotion
  2. Your logo on the Farmhouse Conf 5 section of The Farmhouse website
  3. Your logo on screen in the Farmhouse Conf 5 videos
  4. Thanked on mic to the attendees before that speaker's talk
  5. Totally righteous street cred for sponsoring the final show
